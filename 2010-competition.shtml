2010 Intercollegiate Competition

MIT and BU co-hosted a USFS Intercollegiate Competition at Johnson Ice Rink.

Overall, BU placed first as a tean with 113 total points, followed by University of Delaware in second with 106 points, and Dartmouth in third with 88 points.
