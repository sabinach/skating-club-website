Intercollegiate Competitions

Students have the opportunity to compete at USFS Intercollegiate Competitions each year as part of the MIT FSC. More information, including a list of this year's competitions, can be found here.

Every few years, the MIT FSC hosts an intercollegiate competition at Johnson Ice Rink. Check out some of our past competitions:

  • 2012 MIT Intercollegiate Competition
  • 2010 MIT Intercollegiate Competition

Winter and Spring Exhibitions

The FSC hosts an exhibition in each winter and spring for the MIT community, friends and family. All club members are invited to participate in the opening group number. Many of our skaters also perform individual programs, group ice dances, and Theater on Ice.

Our exhibitions for the 2019-2020 Season will be held on:

  • Winter: Sunday, December 8, 2019
  • Spring: Saturday, March 14, 2020

Check out videos from our past performances in the following YouTube playlists:

  • Spring 2018 Exhibition: SKATE
  • Winter 2017 Exhibition: Dragon Night
  • Spring 2017 Exhibition: Born to Make History
  • Spring 2016 Exhibition

Skating-in-Socks Social

FSC has a pre-rink opening celebration to introduce new members to the club and simulate some figure skating moves off the ice before the rink opens. Thanks to everyone who came this year!

Workshop with JoJo Starbuck!

JoJo Starbuck (3-time US National Pairs Skating Champions and twice Olympic competitor) led a skating workshop with the MITFSC members. Thank you JoJo for inspiring skaters of all levels!
