Lessons

The MIT FSC offers group lessons in freestyle and ice dance and can help people find a private coach. MIT FSC classes cannot count for PE credit. However, DAPER offers some PE classes.

Private Lessons

These coaches currently offer private lessons.

We offer subsidized private lessons to students. If you are interested, please review the rules and procedures.

If you are a private coach interested in giving lessons, please email us at: skating-officers [at] mit [dot] edu.

PE Lessons

While skating with the MIT FSC unfortunately does not count for PE credit, there are basic PE skating classes taught by the Athletics Department in the 2nd quarter, IAP, and 3rd quarter. There is also a figure skating class (prerequisite is the basic skating PE class) offered during IAP. Check the MIT Department of Athletics website for more details.
