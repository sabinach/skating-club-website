2010 Intercollegiate Competition

The MIT and BU co-hosted USFSA intercollegiate competition was held at MIT.

Overall, BU placed first as a school with 113 total points followed by University of Delaware with 106 in second, and Dartmouth in third with 88. Here is a pdf version of the school point totals.
