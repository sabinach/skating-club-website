Welcome

Our club provides an enjoyable, sociable, supportive, and safe environment for figure skaters of all levels. Beginners are always welcome! Membership is open to all members of the MIT community.

The Club offers:

  • Ice time for freestyle, ice dance, moves-in-the-field, pairs, and synchronized skating
  • Weekly groups lessons for members
    • Figure Skating (Freestyle) and Basic Skills
    • Ice Dancing
    • Edge Class/Synchronized Skating
  • Private Instruction
  • Annual skating show
  • Intercollegiate Competitions
  • Social events open to the MIT community

The MIT rink is located in the Johnson Athletic Center.

***Please note that only club members are allowed to practice during MIT Figure Skating Club practice sessions. However, you are always welcome to come, watch and see if you would like to join the club.
